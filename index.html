Welcome to Signlingo

This is a website designed to help the deaf and those interested in ASL. It has a variety of different learning resources, from a dictionary where people can learn their numbers and alphabets to common words and phrases, providing a fun learning experience for people of all ages.

The History of ASL

American Sign Language (ASL) was formally invented in 1817 when Thomas Hopkins Gallaudet and Laurent Clerc founded the American School for the Deaf. It evolved by combining French Sign Language with local American signs, creating the standardized language used today.
