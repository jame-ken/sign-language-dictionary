Welcome to Signlingo

Signlingo is an interactive platform dedicated to bridging the communication gap between the hearing and Deaf communities. We provide high-quality ASL resources for learners of all ages.

The Evolution of ASL

ASL began in 1817 and is a blend of French Sign Language and local American signs. It is a unique language with its own grammar and syntax.

Why Learn ASL?

Learning ASL helps you connect with a vibrant culture, improves brain function, and makes you a more expressive communicator.
