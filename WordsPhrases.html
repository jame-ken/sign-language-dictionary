Greetings & Basics

Hello

Move your flat hand from your forehead outward in a salute-like motion.

Please

Place your flat palm on your chest and move it in a circular motion.

Thank You

Touch your fingertips to your chin and move your hand forward.

Sorry

Make a fist and move it in a circular motion over your heart.

Yes

Make a fist and "nod" it up and down like a head.

No

Snap your index and middle finger down against your thumb.

Goodbye

A simple open-palm wave.

Good Morning

Sign "Good" then move your dominant hand up from the "horizon" of your other arm.

Good Night

Sign "Good" then arch your hand over the other arm like the sun setting.

Want

Bring both hands toward your body with palms up and fingers slightly clawed.

Excuse Me

Brush the fingertips of your dominant hand across the palm of your non-dominant hand.

I Love You

The universal sign combining the letters I, L, and Y.

Family & Pets

Mother

Tap your thumb to your chin with an open hand.

Father

Tap your thumb to your forehead with an open hand.

Brother

Combine the signs for 'Boy' (cap grab) and 'Same'.

Sister

Combine the signs for 'Girl' (jaw stroke) and 'Same'.

Uncle

Shake a "U" handshape near your temple.

Aunt

Shake an "A" handshape near your jaw.

Grandmother

Sign "Mother" but bounce the hand forward away from the chin twice.

Grandfather

Sign "Father" but bounce the hand forward away from the forehead twice.

Dog

Slap your thigh and then snap your fingers.

Cat

Use your thumb and index finger to mimic whiskers on your cheek.

Daily Actions

Eat

Tap your mouth with a flattened "O" handshape.

Drink

Mimic holding and drinking from a cup.

Study

Wiggle your fingers above your other palm as if reading intensely.

Sleep

Bring your hand down from your forehead to your chin as your eyes close.

Potty

Shake a "T" handshape back and forth.

Shower

Open and close your hand above your head to mimic spraying water.

Brush Teeth

Mimic the motion of brushing your teeth with your index finger.

Common Phrases & Objects

I am hungry

Slide your hand in a "C" shape down the center of your chest.

Where is the bathroom?

Shake the "T" handshape while using a questioning facial expression.

What time is it?

Tap your wrist where a watch would normally be.

I don't know

Touch your forehead and then flick your hand away.

Again, please?

Arch your dominant hand into the palm of your other hand.
