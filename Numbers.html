34 Essential Signs

A comprehensive library of core vocabulary and everyday actions.

Greetings & Basics

Hello

Standard greeting. Move your flat hand from your forehead outward.

Please

Circular motion with a flat palm on the chest.

Thank You

Move fingertips from chin forward.

Sorry

Circular motion with a fist (A-handshape) on the chest.

Yes

Nod your fist up and down like a head nodding.

No

Snap index and middle finger down to the thumb.

Goodbye

A standard wave motion.

Good Morning

Sign "Good" then move the arm up like a rising sun.

Good Night

Sign "Good" then arch your hand over the other arm like a setting sun.

Want

Pull your hands toward your body with clawed fingers.

Excuse Me

Brush fingertips of one hand across the palm of the other.

I Love You

The combination of letters I, L, and Y.

Family & Pets

Mother

Thumb to chin with open fingers.

Father

Thumb to forehead with open fingers.

Brother

Combination of "Boy" and "Same."

Sister

Combination of "Girl" and "Same."

Uncle

Shake a "U" handshape near the temple.

Aunt

Shake an "A" handshape near the jaw.

Grandmother

Sign "Mother" but bounce the hand forward twice.

Grandfather

Sign "Father" but bounce the hand forward twice.

Dog

Snap fingers or pat your leg.

Cat

Pinch thumb and index near cheek like whiskers.

Daily Actions

Eat

Tap your mouth with a squished "O" handshape.

Drink

Mimic holding and drinking from a cup.

Study

Wiggle fingers over the other palm like reading intensely.

Sleep

Pull your hand down over your face as eyes close.

Potty

Shake a "T" handshape.

Shower

Open and close hand above head like water spraying.

Brush Teeth

Mimic brushing teeth with index finger.

Phrases & Objects

Hungry

Slide a "C" hand down the center of the chest.

Bathroom

Shake a "T" handshape (Similar to Potty).

Time

Tap your wrist where a watch would be.

Don't Know

Touch forehead and flick hand away with a "know" motion.

Again

Arch fingertips into the other palm.
